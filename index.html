About Us

Meet Mighty Dog Roofing

Mighty Dog Roofing of Greater Middlesex County was founded by Vish Mazumder and Rachel Whitaker after experiencing firsthand the frustration of unreliable contractors during a home improvement project. That experience became our mission: to ensure no homeowner ever has to go through the same thing.

Today, we're a locally owned and operated roofing company serving Westford, Chelmsford, Acton, and over 30 surrounding communities in Middlesex County, MA. We combine national-franchise resources with genuine local care, delivering exceptional craftsmanship, high-caliber materials, and a five-year workmanship warranty on every project.
