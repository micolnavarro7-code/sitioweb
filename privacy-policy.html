This website is owned and operated by Mighty Dog Roofing DBA MDR United LLC. This Privacy Policy explains what personal data is collected through our website and mobile applications (collectively, the "Website"), how that data is used, and your rights regarding your personal information.

By accessing or using this Website, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use. We reserve the right to update this policy by posting changes to the Website. Changes become effective when posted; your continued use of the Website indicates acceptance of any updates.

Your Security

We take commercially reasonable physical, electronic, and administrative steps to maintain the security of your personal information. However, no internet transmission can be guaranteed 100% secure, and we disclaim liability for breaches beyond our control, including hacking or unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Depending on your state of residence, you may have the right to:

  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal data (unless we are legally required to maintain it)
  • Obtain copies of your personal information for transfer to other companies
  • Opt out of targeted advertising and SMS messaging
  • Opt out of profiling

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@mightydog.com.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information in two ways: directly from you and through automated means.

Personal Information You Provide

  • Contact details (name, email, telephone, address)
  • Login and account information (user ID, password)
  • Payment or credit card information through third-party processors

We collect this information when you sign up for email lists, send communications, register accounts, book appointments or services, or participate in promotions, surveys, or contests.

Automated Information

We collect data through cookies, including:

  • IP addresses and geographic location information
  • Device identifiers and characteristics
  • Browser and operating system information
  • Language preferences
  • Referral source websites
  • Actions taken on the Website
  • Visit dates, times, and accessed pages

How We Use Your Information

  • Service Provision: Maintaining accounts and communicating about inquiries
  • Communications: Sending promotional offers, updates, and product information
  • Advertising: Serving targeted advertisements based on website interactions
  • Security: Detecting incidents and protecting against fraud or illegal activity
  • Business Improvement: Troubleshooting, developing features, and analyzing user interactions
  • Legal Compliance: Responding to subpoenas and governmental authorities

Sharing Personal Information with Third Parties

We do not share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes except as outlined in this policy. Text messaging opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

We may share information with:

  • Affiliates or Franchisees: Affiliated brands owned by the same parent company
  • Service Providers: Companies assisting in operations, including database managers, email distributors, advertisers, data storage providers, and fraud prevention services
  • Law Enforcement: When legally required or to cooperate with investigations

Targeted Advertising and Remarketing

We work with third-party advertisers for remarketing across websites and applications. These advertisers track online activities through cookies, pixels, and web beacons to show tailored advertisements. Some advertisers are members of the Network Advertising Initiative (networkadvertising.org).

Your Choices

  • Tracking: You can set your browser to refuse cookies or alert when they are sent. However, disabling cookies may make some features unavailable.
  • Promotional Offers: You can opt out of promotional emails by clicking "unsubscribe" links in emails. Changes take up to ten business days.
  • Advertising: You can opt out through the Digital Advertising Alliance consumer choice page or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out tool.

SMS Terms of Service

By opting in to SMS communications, you consent to receive marketing and informational messages including appointment reminders, schedule change notifications, and promotions. Message frequency varies; message and data rates may apply.

  • Opt-in keyword: START
  • Opt-out keyword: STOP
  • Help keyword: HELP

SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with third parties. By providing your mobile number, you agree to receive recurring promotional and telemarketing messages via automatic dialing systems. Consent is not required for purchase. You may reply STOP at any time.

Analytics

We use the following analytics services:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks usage patterns, IP addresses, and visit frequency using cookies and web beacons.
  • Microsoft Clarity: Captures site usage through behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and session replay.
  • Meta: Personal information and website behavior may be shared with Meta companies for consistent experiences and advertising.
  • Constant Contact: Used for email marketing analytics.
  • HubSpot: Uses cookies and usage data to track website use and analyze demographics.

Children

This Website does not knowingly permit users under 16 years old or knowingly collect their information. Upon discovering a user is underage, we will not use the information without parental consent and will delete it from our records.

Deletion of Your Personal Information

You may request deletion of your personal information by emailing privacy@mightydog.com. We will not delete information if we are legally obligated to maintain it.
